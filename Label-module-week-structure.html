Module and Weeks' Structure

The module is divided into 6 weeks. Each week you will be assigned activities such as reading, lectures, case studies, quizzes and discussion forums. For each week, we recommend that you complete the different activities in the order that they are listed in the syllabus going from top to bottom.

A new week starts every Tuesday at 10h00 am. Refer to the Module Calendar for more details. The Module Calendar displays the key dates the module, including release dates, exam dates and activities submission dates. Ensure that you take careful note of these dates and keep the calendar on hand throughout the module.
