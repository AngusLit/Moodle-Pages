Discussion Forums

A big part of this course is going to be the discussion forums. Each topic includes a discussion forum that facilitates interaction between you, your fellow students, and your teaching team. In these forums you will be able to post:

  • Content and assessment related questions

Please post any questions related to content or assessments here

  • Report technical problems

Any issues you may experience when interacting in/with the MyLMS can be posted here and we will assist you.

  • Interact with your student success coaches

Your student success team will post a welcome and discussion questions designed to facilitate sharing of study strategies and sustenance of energy levels and motivation.

Refer to the Discussion Forum Guide for more information on how to use the group discussion forum.

As you get started with the module, read a welcome message from your module lecturer in the General discussion forum.
